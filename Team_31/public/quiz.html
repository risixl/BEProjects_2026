Mental Health Assessment

Answer the following questions to help us understand your mental health needs.

For each question, select the option that best describes how you've been feeling over the last 2 weeks.

1. How often do you feel that life is meaningless or that you lack purpose?

2. Do you feel disconnected from your emotions, as if you’re just going through the motions?

3. Have you felt an overwhelming sense of sadness, guilt, or worthlessness that you can’t explain?

4. Do you experience mood swings, where you feel fine one moment and deeply upset the next?

5. Do you feel like no one truly understands what you’re going through?

6. Have you noticed any significant changes in your routine, such as neglecting personal hygiene or responsibilities?

7. How often do you cancel plans or avoid social interactions because you feel too exhausted or uninterested?

8. Do you find it difficult to complete even simple tasks, like making a meal or responding to messages?

9. How often do you procrastinate or avoid making decisions due to a lack of motivation?

10. Have you lost interest in hobbies or activities that once brought you joy?

11. Do you often feel physically drained, even after getting enough sleep?

12. Have you been experiencing frequent headaches, stomach issues, or other unexplained body pains?

13. Do you struggle with extreme fatigue or find it hard to get out of bed in the morning?

14. Have you noticed drastic changes in your appetite (overeating or loss of appetite)?

15. Have you been using substances (alcohol, drugs, excessive caffeine) as a coping mechanism?

16. Do you often find yourself lost in negative thoughts that you can’t escape from?

17. How often do you feel overwhelmed by regret or mistakes from your past?

18. Do you struggle with concentration or feel like your mind is constantly foggy?

19. Have you noticed an increase in self-critical or self-destructive thoughts?

20. Do you feel that your relationships with family or friends are deteriorating?

21. How frequently do you experience feelings of worthlessness or guilt?

22. Do you find yourself withdrawing socially or avoiding social interactions?

23. Have you experienced thoughts about self-harm or suicide?

24. Do you feel persistent sadness that doesn't seem to lift?

25. Do you feel like you have no hope for the future?

26. What is your age?
